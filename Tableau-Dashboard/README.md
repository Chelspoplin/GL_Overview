# GL 2024 Overview Dashboard

## Overview

The **GL 2024 Overview Dashboard** provides a high-level financial summary of Revenue, Costs, Profit, and Overall Profit Margin for the selected year. It allows leadership and operational teams to quickly understand performance trends and drill into specific breakdowns by account, department, or description.

This dashboard is designed to answer two primary questions:

1. How are we performing overall?
2. Where is revenue or cost concentration occurring?

---

## Key Metrics (Top Summary Cards)

- **Revenue** – Total revenue for the selected filters
- **Costs** – Total expenses for the selected filters
- **Profit** – Revenue minus Costs
- **Overall PM** – Overall Profit Margin %

These KPI cards provide an immediate snapshot of company health.

---

## Profit by Month

This section visualizes:

- **Debit (Costs)**
- **Credit (Revenue)**
- **Profit Margin % (line overlay)**

### What this shows:
- Monthly revenue vs expense trends
- Margin fluctuations throughout the year
- Seasonality or volatility
- Months with abnormal cost spikes
- Months with unusually high or low margin %

### How it’s useful:
- Identify operational inefficiencies
- Spot margin compression early
- Evaluate performance consistency across months
- Support forecasting and budgeting conversations

---

## GL Amounts by Breakout

This visual dynamically breaks down GL amounts based on user selection.

### Controls:
- **Account Type** (Revenue or Cost)
- **Select Breakout** (Account Name, Description, or Department)

### What this shows:
- Revenue concentration by department
- Cost concentration by expense category
- Department-level performance
- High-impact GL categories

### How it’s useful:
- Identify largest cost drivers
- Evaluate revenue mix (Sales vs Install vs Service, etc.)
- Support cost reduction strategies
- Guide resource allocation decisions
- Prepare leadership-ready breakdowns

---

## Filters & Navigation

The dashboard includes filters for:

- Account Name
- Description
- Department

These allow users to isolate specific business segments.

A **“Go to Details”** button provides navigation to a deeper-level analysis dashboard for transaction-level review.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard supports:

- Executive performance reviews
- Budget planning discussions
- Margin analysis
- Department-level accountability
- Strategic cost management
- Revenue mix optimization

It provides both high-level visibility and flexible breakdown capability without requiring SQL-level analysis.
